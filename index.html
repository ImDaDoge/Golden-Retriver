Golden Retriever

All you need to know about Golden Retrievers

The Golden Retriever is one of the most popular dog breeds in the United States.
They’re known for being friendly and intelligent making them the perfect family dog and good service dogs.
They’re very easy to train and live for 10-12 years, they’re also very protective of their owners.

Here are some things you need to know!

Weight range:

Male: 29-34kg
Female: 25-29kg

Expectations:

Exercise Requirements: >40 minutes/day
Energy Level:avrage
Longevity Range 10-13 years
Tendency to Drool: Low
Tendency to Snore: Low
Tendency to Bark: Moderate
Tendency to Dig: Low
Social/Attention Needs: High
Overall Grooming Needs: Moderate

Personality

The Golden Retriever is well tempered, intelligent and affectionate. Golden Retrievers are playful but gentle towards children.
They tend to get along well with other pets and strangers. These dogs are eager to please, which may explain why they respond so
well to obedience training and are popular service dogs. They also like to work, whether it is hunting birds or bringing guardian slippers.
Golden Retrievers are usually not skinners, they lack the instinct of a guard, so don't expect them to be good watchdogs. However, some
golden retrievers will notify you when a stranger approaches.
